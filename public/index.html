Official Member Card Verification

Verify Society Member Card

To verify a card, either scan the QR with your mobile device or use the roll number lookup.

Verify using QR code

1. Open the camera or QR-scanner app on your mobile phone.
2. Point it at the QR code on the AU CSSM / AU Cyber Security Society member card.
3. Your phone will open a secure verification link.
4. If the card is valid, the member details will be shown.

Verify using Roll Number

Enter a roll number to verify a member.
